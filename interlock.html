Interlock Cleaning

Our professional interlock cleaning process begins with the application of high-quality interlock cleaning solutions specifically formulated for different types of interlock surfaces. These specialized cleaners effectively break down dirt, grime, and stubborn stains. After applying the cleaner, we use professional-grade scrubbing tools and techniques to thoroughly agitate and remove accumulated debris, water spots, and mineral deposits. The final step involves meticulously wiping and polishing the interlock to achieve a streak-free, crystal-clear finish that enhances the transparency and appearance of your interlock surfaces.

Heavy Machinery

Our specialized heavy machinery utilizes powerful pressure washing and mechanical scrubbing systems to effectively clean interlock brick surfaces. The industrial-grade equipment applies controlled high-pressure water jets combined with rotating brush attachments to dislodge and remove deeply embedded dirt, moss, weeds, and other debris from between the bricks. Our professional machines are calibrated to deliver optimal cleaning power while protecting the structural integrity of the interlocking pavers. The combination of pressure washing and mechanical agitation ensures thorough cleaning of both the brick surfaces and the joints between them, restoring the original appearance of your interlock installation.
